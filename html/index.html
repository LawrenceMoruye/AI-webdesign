Branches of Artificial Intelligence

Machine learning

Field of study that gives computers the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed. A computer program is said to learn from experience E with respect to some class of tasks T andperformance measure P, if its performance at tasks in T, as measured by P, improves with experience E.There are various types of machine learning that we'll cover.They include:supervised learming,unsurpervised learning and reinforcement learning.

Deep Learning

It is a specific subfield of machine learning that puts an emphasis on learning successive layers of increasingly meaningful representantions.Deep in deep learning is hidden layers.Neural networks are used here whose function is to receive input perform some computation and produce output.some Deep learning algorithms include:convolutional neural networks,recurrent neural networks,RNM and DBN.

Data Science

It is a science related to data.In general data science is the process of deriving knowledge and insights from a huge and diverse set of data through organizing,processing and analysing the data.Data is the modern gold and should be well collected,cleaned and stored.Wrong data leads to wrong insights.Accurate,timely and relevant data is key to good decision making.It has applications in recommendation systems.
